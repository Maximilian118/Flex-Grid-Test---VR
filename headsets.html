Oculus Quest

After an explanation of the latest hardware Oculus intend on releasing Adam Savage Tested have a chat with Product Manager Sean Liu about the hardware inside the up and coming Oculus Quest.

The Quest will receive a resolution upgrade to 1440-1600 whilst keeping the Oled colours Rift owners love as well as making sensors redundant with sensors built into the headset. Oh, and it's cordless!

Popular Headsets

Oculus Rift

The Rift is without doubt the most popular VR headset to date. That's not to say it doesn't have any competition though with the HTC Vive nipping at its heals. Further inovation is an absolute must if Oculus wish to stay king of the hill.

The rift contains a luscious 1080x1200 per eye 90Hz Oled and is one of the most polished, premium headsets on the market today. For a 'true VR' 360 degree experiance the rift is one of the best options out there.

Full Article

HTC Vive

Similaraly to the Oculus rift, the HTC Vive has posesses a 1080x1200 per eye 80Hz PenTile Oled display. Contrastingly the vive uses a lighthouse system for motion tracking which boasts a more acurate, bigger potential play area at the detrimant of a larger price tag.

The vive isn't without its critisisms however. The controllers are slated to be heavy and unnatural in the players hand, the content library is much to be desired and due to the extra £100+ over the rift sales aren't up to par with the competition.
